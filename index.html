Notes:

Priority Level:

  • High: Issues that affect safety, immediate customer satisfaction, or operational functionality.
  • Medium: Important feedback that should be addressed promptly but does not immediately impact operations.
  • Low: Suggestions or non-critical issues that can be reviewed and addressed in routine cycles.

Accountable Team:

Should be the team with the most expertise or responsibility for the area of concern.
